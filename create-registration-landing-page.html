Create a registration

First time users may take 15-30 minutes depending on the type of registration you need to create.

Fees start at $6.00 for a 7 year registration.

If you need to come back later, save as pending on any page. You'll get a pending application number or you can enter your own reference number. You'll then have 7 days to complete your registration

Before you start

Before you start your application to register a security interest in collateral, check that you have the information that you need. A list of the information that you require to create the registration is outlined below.

You will need to enter details about:

Secured Party

Collateral

Grantor

You or your organisation are likely to be the secured party.

The personal property you are registering (such as a motor vehicle).

The party you are registering against.

Registration process

Maintaining registrations

As the PPSR is a real-time noticeboard of security interests, available to users 24/7, making sure the information is current and up to date is essential for effective searching.

Some considerations to keep in mind:

  • Review registrations with no stated end date.
  • Extend registrations before they expire.
  • Keep your details up to date.
  • Build in systems and controls to manage key points of the registrations life cycle.
  • Read more on responsible PPSR registration management on the website.
