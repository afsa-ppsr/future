Collateral type

Consumer property: Generally any property held by an individual that isn't used for commercial purposes. This means it's not being used to carry on a business under an ABN.

Commercial property: If the property doesn't meet the description of consumer property, then it's likely commercial property.

For more information, visit the PPSR website.



Transitional type

Important: Selecting the "transitional" radio button in respect of collateral that is not covered by a transitional security agreement will render the registration ineffective with respect to that collateral.



Secured party group details

You will need to enter your SPG number. You can create an SPG, if you don not have one.

An SPG contains the details of the individuals or organisations with an interest in the property. A secured party is also known as: lender, lessor, consignor, financier, or seller.
