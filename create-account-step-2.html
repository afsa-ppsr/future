Account payment type

Select account payment type



Pay as you go: Pay for each transaction by credit card (Visa, MasterCard or American Express).

Pre-pay: Add funds to your account at any time by credit card or EFT. Payment for each transaction will be deducted from the available balance.

Frequent users can apply for credit after creating the account. To apply for credit, you will need to complete the AFSA credit application form.

For more information, visit the PPSR website.

Send financial statements/tax invoices

Select the frequency for sending financial statements





Pay as you go accounts must select between receiving tax invoices by email for every transaction or never.

Pre-pay accounts must select between the monthly statement options.

Tax invoices can be downloaded when needed.
