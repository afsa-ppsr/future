The grantor is the individual or organisation you are registering against (e.g. your customer, debtor, lesee or borrower.

Important: The PPS Act creates strict requirements for how grantors are identified.

Entering the wrong grantor details can have serious consequences including the risk of your registration not being effective.

Vist our website for more information on selecting the grantor.

-

Grantor 1

Grantor type

Consumer property: Generally any property held by an individual that isn't used for commercial purposes. This means it's not being used to carry on a business under an ABN.

Commercial property: If the property doesn't meet the description of consumer property, then it's likely commercial property.

For more information, visit the PPSR website.
