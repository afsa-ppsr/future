Create an account

Creating an account is free and for a first-time user, it takes approximately 10-15 minutes to do.

What you will need

To set up an account, you will need:

  • Your or your organisation's contact details.
  • A person to nominate as a key contact - they will also be the account administrator.
  • To read and accept the account customer terms and conditions.

Benefits of creating an account

You don't need to create an account to do a search. But if you are a regular searcher, you will have access to the following benefits:

  • Flexible payment options.
  • Access to transaction history.

Manage PPSR transactions

A PPSR account lets you create and manage registrations and view your previous transactions.

Account administrators and users

Your account can have one or more users. The first user created will be the administrator who can assist other users with password rests and resetting SPG access codes.

PPSR transaction fees

You will need to choose a payment method to be used for searches, registrations and other transactions that require a fee. The fees can be found on the PPSR website.
