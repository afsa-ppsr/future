A PPSR account lets you create and manage PPSR registrations and view your previous transactions.

The first user created will be the administrator, they can make changes to the account and create users.

For more information, visit the PPSR website.


Account name

The account customer name is the name of your organisation or an individual name.

The name recorded here will appear on all invoices and statements. You can change the name at any time.



Mailing address










The mailing address will appear on all invoices and statements.

Identification

Organisation identifier type:

The organisation identifier will appear on all invoices and statements. You cannot change an identifier after you create the account.

Contact details









Record the details of the person who will manage the account, known as the administrator.

The administrator can add more users or administrators after creating the account.
